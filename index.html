What is the SubPos Wi-Fi Positoning Standard?

The SubPos Wi-Fi Positoning Standard is a dataless positioning system that doesn't require expensive licencing, specialised hardware, laborious area profiling or reliance on data connectivity (a connection to a location database or cellphone coverage is not required), all while remaining backwards compatible with existing Wi-Fi access points and receivers. It works independently of, or alongside GPS/Wi-Fi Positioning Systems (WPS)/Indoor Positioning Systems (IPS) as an additional positioning data source by exploiting hardware commonly available.

Why SubPos Wi-Fi Standard?

  1. Backwards compatible with a vast majority of devices.
  2. Very fast acquisition time (~2 secs).
  3. Low location latency.
  4. No data connectivity required, so no giving your position to big data.

The SubPos Ranger

The SubPos Ranger allows you to accurately measure distance or obtain your position indoors for all hobbyist robotics applications.

Comparison to iBeacon

  1. No database connection required.
  2. Works with existing infrastructure.
  3. Larger range.
  4. No hardware or vendor lock-in.
